Introduction

There are a few disadvantages to the wonderful experience of living in space, specifically the physical toll it takes on an astronaut's body.

Isolated and confined for a long time far away from everything they've ever known. The stress caused by their jobs and the threats around them causes many problems which affect an astronaut's health deeply and can even interfere with their work. Neurospace is sure to reduce stress levels to the classical techniques of massage and music relaxation. It also helps astronauts sleep better, this machine sure is a dream.
